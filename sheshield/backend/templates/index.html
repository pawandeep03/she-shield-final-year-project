Safety Guidelines

How to use the prediction and what to do in each risk level

Low Risk

  • Share your live location with a trusted person.
  • Keep phone charged and emergency contacts saved.
  • Prefer well-lit routes even in safe areas.

Medium Risk

  • Avoid isolated shortcuts; choose main roads.
  • Travel with a friend when possible.
  • Stay alert and keep your surroundings in mind.

High Risk

  • Avoid the area if possible, especially at night.
  • Inform family/friends before travelling.
  • Keep Police (100) and emergency numbers ready.
